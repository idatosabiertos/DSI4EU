Nesta has a really long name and looks like this

Nesta is an innovation charity with a mission to help people and organisations bring great ideas to life

We are dedicated to supporting ideas that can help improve all our lives, with activities ranging from early stage investment to in-depth research and practical programmes.

We don’t work alone - we rely on the strength of the partnerships we form with you and with others to make change happen. - See more at: http://www.nesta.org.uk/about-us

Social Impact

DSI4EU will support, grow and scale the current Digital Social Innovation network of projects, organisations, and individuals bringing together social entrepreneurs, hackers, communities and academics working on key DSI fields such as the maker movement, the collaborative economy, open democracy and digital rights.
