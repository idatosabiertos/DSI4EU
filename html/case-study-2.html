Goteo

INTRODUCTION

Goteo.org is an open source social network for crowdfunding and distributed collaboration. Their mission is to help finance and support innovative initiatives that contribute to the common good, free knowledge, and open code. They aim to facilitate the collective investment in open projects with social, cultural, scientific, educational, technological or ecological objectives that generate new opportunities for the improvement of society.

Overview

Goteo.org is an open source social network for crowdfunding as well as distributed collaboration based in Spain. The explicit mission of Goteo.org is to help finance and support “the independent development of creative and innovative initiatives that contribute to the common good, free knowledge, and open code”. Building on this, the platform aims to facilitate ‘“the collective investment in open projects with social, cultural, scientific, educational, technological or ecological objectives that generate new opportunities for the improvement of society and the enrichment of community goods and resources”.

Goteo helps communities and projects connected with media activism, ecology, urban transformation and communal action, so they can share their knowledge with society, find collaborative resources using crowdfunding and facilitate the collaboration of citizens, public institutions and companies. Some other features that differentiate Goteo from most other platforms include the fact that Goteo is backed by a non-profit (Goteo Foundation); crowdsourcing and the lending of skills and tools are welcome - hence Goteo is not solely focussed on money; and the fact that they hold institutionally endorsed workshops which provide people access to innovative training methodologies in relation to crowdfunding.

Over the years Goteo has received awards and international recognition, and has replicas and alliances in several countries. Since 2011, Goteo has received over 900 projects, with over 700 of these going on to be published, and a current success rate of 71.05%. The total funds raised by Goteo so far is 3,768,219 EUR.
