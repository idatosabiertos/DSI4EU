DSI4EU

DSI4EU is a support CAPs support action. The aim of the project is to support, gather and grow the DSI community.

DSI4EU fosters digital innovations for the social good, helping communities share data, collaborate to solve problems and scale their initiatives, focusing on open and distributed technologies and new sustainable business models. 

DSI4EU is developing & upgrading the digitalsocial.eu platform, to promote large-scale collaboration and support experimentation among the DSI community and activate collective awareness with a large number of citizens across Europe.

DSI4EU will also provide an engaging programme of training, mentoring, events, policy workshops, and experimentation. DSI4EU will represent the building blocks for a new participatory innovation model for Europe, a more decentralised web and an inclusive and sustainable society, including a radical approach to scaling, extending and connecting the DSI network in Europe.

Social Impact

DSI4EU will support, grow and scale the current Digital Social Innovation network of projects, organisations, and individuals bringing together social entrepreneurs, hackers, communities and academics working on key DSI fields such as the maker movement, the collaborative economy, open democracy and digital rights.
